SandScape - A Digital Sandtray Therapy for Kids

ROLE
Technical Artist

YEAR
2024

TOOLS USED
Unity

GENRE
Web
RESPONSIBILITY
  1. Led the integration and optimization of 8 environments and 132 separate 3D assets (miniatures), working closely with the 3D artist and developers to ensure seamless Unity deployment.
  2. Developed and implemented custom shaders, post-processing, and VFX to enhance visual engagement and immersion
DESCRIPTION
SandScape is an industrial collaboration with BC Children's Hospital Digital Lab, aimed at developing a digital therapeutic tool for kids. As part of an agile team of six students, we employed a user-centered design approach to create a digital sandtray using Unity, which was deployed to WebGL. This tool supports therapeutic practices, helping children through interactive and immersive experiences.

The playable WebGL demo is hosted by the CDM here. It is accessible on both PC and Mac via browser, with mouse controls. While it can also run on mobile browsers, full touchscreen compatibility is not yet supported.
